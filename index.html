What is a Bitcoin credit card?

A Bitcoin-linked card lets you spend BTC anywhere cards are accepted. When you tap or swipe, the provider sells enough BTC to cover the purchase, and the merchant receives local fiat currency.

  • Spend BTC like a normal debit/credit card
  • Auto-conversion at transaction time
  • Rewards may pay out in BTC
  • Works globally on Visa/Mastercard rails

How the BTC → Card flow works

1. Tap or swipe

NFC or chip sends tokenized card data — blockchain is not touched.

2. BTC conversion

Provider sells enough BTC instantly to fund the fiat amount.

3. Visa/Mastercard rails

The payment travels through normal card networks like any other card.

4. Settlement

Merchant receives fiat. Your BTC balance decreases accordingly.
