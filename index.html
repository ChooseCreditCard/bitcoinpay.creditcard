What is a Bitcoin credit card?

A Bitcoin-linked card lets you spend BTC anywhere cards are accepted. When you tap or swipe, the card provider sells enough BTC to cover the purchase, while the merchant receives local currency.

  • • Spend BTC like a normal debit/credit card
  • • Auto-conversion at transaction time
  • • Rewards may pay out in BTC
  • • Works globally on Visa/Mastercard rails

How the BTC → Card flow works

1. You tap or swipe

NFC or chip sends tokenized card data (no blockchain exposure).

2. BTC conversion

Provider sells enough BTC to fund the fiat amount.

3. Visa/Mastercard rails

The transaction moves through traditional card networks.

4. Settlement

Merchant receives fiat. Your BTC balance decreases proportionally.
